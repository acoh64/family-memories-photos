How to Add Your Memory

  1. Fill out the form and click Generate JSON.
  2. Copy the generated JSON entry.
  3. Open data/memories.json in your editor.
  4. Paste the entry into the array (add a comma after the previous entry).
  5. Add your photo file(s) to the photos/ folder.
  6. Commit and push your changes.
